Our Studio

Twenty-five years of design that works

The ethics of aesthetics that work.

For over 25 years, The Right Angle has been an award-winning force in design, shaping transformative environments and products. Our practice is built on a quarter-century of work across the integrated disciplines of interior, product, retail, spatial, and event design.

That guiding principle — the ethics of aesthetics that work — runs through everything our team does. We carry a project from concept to reality under one roof, managing every detail of design, bespoke manufacturing, and final installation. It is a commitment to quality, innovation, and flawless execution that has earned us a discerning clientele in India and abroad.
